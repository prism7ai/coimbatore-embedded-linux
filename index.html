Linux Community Insights
Why Coimbatore? Why Now?
Coimbatore aims to become a leading center for embedded Linux ARM and RISC development , leveraging its engineering talent and growing interest in open-source platforms..
What Makes a Maker Ecosystem Thrive
The Linux Community is a living network that connects engineers, students, educators, and startups to co-create solutions using SOM/SBC platforms, kernel customization, and hardware prototyping.
Technical Talent Meets Real-World R&D
We're fostering collaboration between PSG Tech, KCT, CIT, Simgrosys, and ELCIDD to bridge research and real-world implementation, promoting high-impact learning and product development.
A Day of Demos, Discussions & Discovery
The inaugural Linux meetup attracted over 60 enthusiasts, showcasing live kernel builds and SOM showcase booths, fostering a hands-on celebration of embedded creativity.
Workshops, Talks, and Hackathons
Our community hosts Yocto deep dives, kernel labs, and open-source contribution sprints, catering to beginners and seasoned developers with various sessions available.
Real Impact, Real People — Powered by the Linux Community

Success metrics will focus on meaningful growth indicators including active participation, completed projects, and qualitative feedback from members. Our roadmap includes regional hackathons, partnerships with national Linux groups, and eventual expansion to neighboring cities.
